### Unpublished Works by Known Individual Creators

**Key Question**: When did the creator die?

For unpublished works where you know the individual creator, copyright duration is based on the author's death date.

NOTE: The death year threshold advances by one year every January 1.
As of 2026: died before 1956 = Public Domain; died 1956 or later = In Copyright.

**If Creator Died BEFORE 1956**
→ Go to creation date check (need to verify when work was created)

For creators who died before 1956, the work may be in public domain depending on when it was created:
- If created BEFORE 1906: Public Domain
- If created 1906 or later: Public Domain (because life + 70 years has passed)

**Result for Creator Who Died Before 1956**
- **Status**: Public Domain
- **Term**: Life + 70 years has expired
- **Rights Statement**: No Copyright - United States
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/NoC-US/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: HIGH (if death date confirmed)

**If Creator Died 1956 or Later**
- **Status**: In Copyright
- **Term**: Life of author + 70 years
- **Expiration**: January 1 of the year after 70th anniversary of death
- **Rights Statement**: In Copyright
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: HIGH

**If Creator Is Still Alive**
- **Status**: In Copyright
- **Term**: Will expire 70 years after author's death
- **Rights Statement**: In Copyright
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: HIGH

**If Death Date Is Unknown**
- **Status**: Copyright Undetermined
- **Action**: Research needed to find death date
- **Rights Statement**: Copyright Undetermined
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/
- **Note**: Copyright Office certification of no death record is a defense to infringement

**Examples** (as of 2026)

| Creator Death Date | Work Status | Expires |
|--------------------|-------------|---------|
| 1940 | Public Domain | Already expired |
| 1954 | Public Domain | Already expired |
| 1955 | Public Domain | Expired January 1, 2026 |
| 1956 | In Copyright | January 1, 2027 |
| 1960 | In Copyright | January 1, 2031 |
| 2000 | In Copyright | January 1, 2071 |
| Still living | In Copyright | 70 years after death |

**Special Rule for Pre-1978 Unpublished Works**
Works created before 1978 that remained unpublished were guaranteed protection until at least December 31, 2002. If published before that date, protection extends to at least December 31, 2047.
