### Unpublished Works Where Creator is Unknown

When the creator of an unpublished work cannot be identified, copyright duration is based on the creation date.

**Key Question**: When was this work created?

NOTE: The 120-year threshold advances by one year every January 1.
As of 2026: created before 1906 = Public Domain.

**If Created BEFORE 1906**
- **Status**: Public Domain
- **Reason**: 120 years from creation has passed (2026 − 120 = 1906)
- **Rights Statement**: No Copyright - United States
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/NoC-US/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: HIGH

**If Created 1906-1955**
- **Status**: Copyright Undetermined
- **Reason**: May or may not be in public domain depending on unknown author's death date
- **Rights Statement**: Copyright Undetermined
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: LOW - Research recommended

**If Created 1956 or Later**
- **Status**: In Copyright (assumed)
- **Term**: 120 years from creation
- **Rights Statement**: In Copyright
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC/1.0/
- **Note**: Consider using InC-RUU if reasonable search for creator failed

**If Creation Date is Also Unknown**
- **Status**: Copyright Undetermined
- **Rights Statement**: Copyright Undetermined
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/
- **Confidence Level**: LOW - Cannot make determination

**Special Rights Statement for Unlocatable Rights-Holders**

If you've conducted a reasonable search and cannot identify or locate the creator:
- **Rights Statement**: In Copyright - Rights-holder(s) Unlocatable or Unidentifiable
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC-RUU/1.0/

**What Constitutes a "Reasonable Search"?**
- Check institutional records
- Search copyright registration records
- Search online databases
- Check biographical resources
- Document your search efforts

**Term for Anonymous/Pseudonymous Works**
- 95 years from publication, OR
- 120 years from creation
- Whichever expires first

**If Author's Identity Is Later Revealed**
If the author's identity is revealed in Copyright Office records, the term becomes life + 70 years instead.

**Practical Approach** (as of 2026)

| Creation Date | Recommended Action |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Before 1906 | Treat as public domain |
| 1906-1955 | Research creator identity; if not found, use UND statement |
| 1956+ | Assume in copyright; use InC-RUU if creator search failed |
| Unknown | Cannot determine; use UND statement |
