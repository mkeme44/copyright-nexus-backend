### Unpublished Works Created by Organizations

When an unpublished work was created by or for an organization (corporate authorship), different rules apply.

**Key Question**: Is the organization still operating?

**If Organization Is STILL OPERATING**
- **Status**: In Copyright
- **Term**: 120 years from creation (for unpublished works)
- **Rights Statement**: In Copyright
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC/1.0/
- **Contact**: The organization for permissions

**If Organization Is DEFUNCT**
- **Status**: Copyright Undetermined
- **Reason**: Rights ownership unclear; may have transferred or been abandoned
- **Rights Statement**: Copyright Undetermined
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/
- **Action**: Research needed to determine current rights holder

**If Organization Status Is Unknown**
- **Status**: Copyright Undetermined
- **Rights Statement**: Copyright Undetermined
- **URI**: https://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/
- **Action**: Research the organization's current status

**What Counts as "Corporate Authorship"?**

Works made for hire by organizations include:
- Internal reports and memos
- Institutional photographs
- Organizational records
- Works by employees within scope of employment
- Commissioned works with written work-for-hire agreements

**Researching Defunct Organizations**

When an organization no longer exists:
1. Check if it merged with another organization
2. Look for successor organizations
3. Check dissolution records for asset transfers
4. Research corporate records in state of incorporation
5. Check if copyright was explicitly transferred

**Duration Rules for Corporate Works**

| Work Type | Term |
|-----------|------|
| Unpublished corporate work | 120 years from creation |
| Published corporate work | 95 years from publication OR 120 years from creation (shorter) |

**Example Scenarios**

| Situation | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Internal memo from existing company (1980) | In Copyright until 2100 |
| Records from defunct business (1950) | Copyright Undetermined |
| Photographs from museum that still exists | In Copyright |
| Papers from dissolved nonprofit | Research successor/transfers |

**Note on Government Contractors**
Works created by government contractors are NOT automatically in the public domain. The contractor may hold copyright unless the contract specified otherwise.
