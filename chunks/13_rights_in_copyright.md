### When to Use "In Copyright" Rights Statements

Use these statements when your copyright analysis determines the work IS protected by copyright.

---

## InC - In Copyright (Standard)

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Work is confirmed to be in copyright
- Your organization has determined the copyright status
- You know or can identify the rights holder

**Applies To**:
- Published works 1930-1963 with notice AND renewal confirmed
- Published works 1964-1977 with notice
- Published works 1978-1989 with notice (or cured)
- Published works after March 1989
- Unpublished works by creators who died 1955 or later
- Unpublished works by living creators
- Corporate works still within term

**User Guidance**: "You are free to use this Item in any way that is permitted by the copyright and related rights legislation that applies to your use. For other uses you need to obtain permission from the rights-holder(s)."

---

## InC-EDU - In Copyright - Educational Use Permitted

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC-EDU/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Work is in copyright AND
- Educational use is explicitly permitted without additional permission

**Note**: This requires explicit permission from rights holder for educational use, not just a fair use assumption.

---

## InC-NC - In Copyright - Non-Commercial Use Permitted

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC-NC/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Work is in copyright AND
- Non-commercial use is explicitly permitted

---

## InC-RUU - In Copyright - Rights-holder(s) Unlocatable or Unidentifiable

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/InC-RUU/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Work is reasonably believed to be in copyright
- Reasonable search conducted but rights holder cannot be found
- You've documented your search efforts

**Applies To**:
- Unpublished works with unknown creator (created 1956+)
- Works where original publisher is defunct
- Orphan works (but NOT in EU - use InC-OW-EU there)

**Important**: Requires reasonable effort to locate rights holder. Document your search.

---

## Choosing the Right "In Copyright" Statement

| Situation | Statement |
|-----------|-----------|
| Standard in-copyright, rights holder known | InC |
| In-copyright, educational use granted | InC-EDU |
| In-copyright, non-commercial use granted | InC-NC |
| In-copyright, rights holder not found after search | InC-RUU |
