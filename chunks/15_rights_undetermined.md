### When to Use "Undetermined" Rights Statements

Use these statements when your copyright analysis cannot reach a definitive conclusion.

---

## UND - Copyright Undetermined

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/UND/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Copyright status could NOT be determined despite reasonable effort
- Research was conducted but inconclusive
- Status depends on facts that cannot be established

**Applies To**:
- Published works 1930-1963 where renewal status is unknown
- Published works 1978-1989 without notice, cure status unknown
- Unpublished works where creator death date is unknown
- Unpublished works with unknown creator, created 1905-1955
- Works by defunct organizations (ownership unclear)

**User Guidance**: "The copyright and related rights status of this Item has been reviewed by [Institution], but we were unable to make a conclusive determination."

**Important**: This statement indicates you TRIED to determine status. It's not a default for "we didn't look."

---

## CNE - Copyright Not Evaluated

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/CNE/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Copyright status has NOT been assessed
- No effort has been made to determine status

**Caution**: This should NOT be used as a default placeholder. It indicates the institution has not done any copyright analysis.

---

## NKC - No Known Copyright

**URI**: http://rightsstatements.org/vocab/NKC/1.0/

**Use When**:
- Organization reasonably believes item is NOT restricted by copyright
- BUT conclusive determination could not be made
- Higher confidence than UND, but not definitive

**Do NOT Use For**:
- Orphan works (which are assumed in-copyright)
- Works where you haven't done research

---

## Choosing Between UND, CNE, and NKC

| Situation | Statement | Confidence |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| No copyright analysis done | CNE | N/A |
| Analysis done, couldn't determine | UND | Low |
| Analysis done, probably not in copyright | NKC | Medium |
| Analysis done, confirmed public domain | NoC-US | High |
| Analysis done, confirmed in copyright | InC | High |

---

## When "Undetermined" Results Occur

**Renewal Research Needed (1930-1963)**
- Work has notice but renewal status unknown
- Action: Search renewal records
- If found: Use InC
- If not found: Use NoC-US
- If records unavailable: Stay with UND

**Creator Death Date Unknown (Unpublished)**
- Can't calculate life + 70 years
- Action: Research creator biography
- If found: Apply appropriate rule
- If not found: May use UND or InC-RUU

**Notice Cure Status Unknown (1978-1989)**
- Work published without notice
- Don't know if cure was applied
- Action: Check registration records
- If registered within 5 years: Likely cured, use InC
- If no record: May remain UND

---

## Providing Context with Undetermined Statements

When using UND, consider adding:
- What research was conducted
- Why determination couldn't be made
- What additional research might resolve it
- Contact information for questions
